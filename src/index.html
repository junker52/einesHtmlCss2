The Master's Degree in Website and Web Application Development aims to meet the growing professional demand of specialized front-end web developers, with deep knowledge of markup and layout, mastering the tools used in production and back-end environments.

Given both access profiles and market needs, the online Master of Website and Web Application Development is eminently practical, with client-side development over server-side development. The master's syllabus is divided into three main areas: web design and layout (HTML and CSS and interface design), web development (JavaScript programming, front-end development and back-end development) and tools for design and development.

The master's degree is primarily aimed at application development professionals who want to redirect their web career. Therefore, assume that students have solid programming skills. However, students who do not have previous programming skills will be able to access it, taking a complement of training consisting of two initial subjects in order to learn how to program with JavaScript.

The UAB Degree in Environmental Sciences offers a good background in biology, physics, chemistry, mathematics, engineering, geography and ecology and also deepens in economics, business, political science, law and sociology. It is a single degree relative to the rest of the AC grades in the environment.

The degree includes field and laboratory work, data management and mapping interpretation. It has an internship program in business and several exchange programs with renowned international universities. The 48 credits of optional subjects allow to deepen in more specific areas: the generation of renewable energies, the sustainability of the cities, the climate change, the management of resources and natural spaces, the remediation and conservation, the education, etc.

The UAB CA degree is the most multidisciplinary and most experienced in Spain, with recognized professors and experts in the field and continuity in master's and doctoral studies, both within one of the best international research centers. in environmental science, the Institute of Environmental Science and Technology (ICTA), as in other UAB research groups.
